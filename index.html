Privacy Policy

Ahmad Habboubah built the Pizza Driver Log app as a free app. This SERVICE is provided at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page explains how Pizza Driver Log handles information, since your privacy matters.

Summary

Pizza Driver Log does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on any server. There is no backend and no account system. Everything you enter — trips, cash and tips, fuel entries, odometer readings, and settings — is saved only in your device's local app storage and never leaves your device unless you export it yourself.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not request, collect, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All data you enter is stored locally on your device using standard browser/app local storage. I (the developer) have no access to this data, no server it is sent to, and no way to view, recover, or delete it on your behalf — it exists only on your device.

Third-Party Services

Pizza Driver Log does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app does not use cookies. Your device's operating system (Android/iOS) and app store (Google Play) may collect standard installation and diagnostic information under their own privacy policies, which are outside this app's control:

  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy

Device Backups

If your device's operating system backup feature is enabled (e.g. Android auto-backup to your own Google account), your locally stored app data may be included in that backup. This is controlled entirely by you and your device/account settings — the developer has no access to these backups and no data is ever sent to the developer's own servers, because none exist.

Data Deletion

Since all data lives only on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's storage/data or uninstalling the app. There is nothing stored elsewhere to delete.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13, because it does not collect information from anyone at all.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if the app's data practices change. Any changes will be posted on this page.

This policy is effective as of 2026-07-01.
